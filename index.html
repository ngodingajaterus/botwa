Converts Text into Images

Almost all the messages you posted on Internet are unsecure and accessible by everyone around the world. Search engines, robots, crawlers are collecting this information daily and indexed in their database. They make all your private information such as e-mail address, phone number, personal information searchable by the world.

This information provides a good opportunity for spammers or scammers to make you their victim. One easiest way to prevent these, is by converting your information into images before publish on Internet. IMGBun is built on this purpose to convert text into .gif, .jpg and .png images to protect your online privacy!
